Our Story

Where it began

Two friends.
One Sunday morning.

Pori Cardiff began with two friends, a Sunday morning, and a conviction that Cardiff deserved somewhere worth getting out of bed for. Sam Hooper and Ross Holland met working the same kitchen, talking for years about the kind of place they would open if they ever got the chance. Trade Street gave them that chance.

The space is warm and unhurried. The cooking is honest. Everything on the table has been thought about.

A place that rewards those who take their time.

Good food does not announce itself. It just stays with you.
