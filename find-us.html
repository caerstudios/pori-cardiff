Directions

Getting Here

On foot

10 minutes from Cardiff Central station. Follow Bute Street south, then turn onto Trade Street.

By bus

Routes 8 and 9 stop on Bute Street, a 3 minute walk from Trade Street.

Parking

Mermaid Quay car park is the closest option, a 5 minute walk from the restaurant.

By bike

Nextbike docking station available on Bute Street, a short ride from Trade Street.
