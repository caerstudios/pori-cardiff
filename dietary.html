Pori is for everyone. Here is what you need to know.

v

Vegetarian

Dishes marked v are suitable for vegetarians. No meat or fish. May contain eggs and dairy.

vg

Vegan

Dishes marked vg are suitable for vegans. No animal products. We can also adjust many dishes on request.

In our kitchen

Allergens

Our kitchen handles all 14 major allergens including gluten, dairy, eggs, nuts, fish and shellfish. Whilst we take care to avoid cross-contamination, we cannot guarantee any dish is completely allergen-free. Please speak to a member of the team before ordering if you have a severe allergy.

  • Celery
  • Cereals containing gluten
  • Crustaceans
  • Eggs
  • Fish
  • Lupin
  • Milk
  • Molluscs
  • Mustard
  • Peanuts
  • Sesame
  • Soya
  • Sulphur dioxide
  • Tree nuts
