Pori is a brunch and sando bar on Trade Street, Cardiff. We cook with care: seasonal produce, big flavour, and the kind of attention that makes a Saturday morning worth getting out of bed for.

Whether you are staying for a long spread or grabbing a quick sando on your way through the city, every plate earns your time. Cosy without compromise.
